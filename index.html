Technology

  • Netra's appearance-based tracking and modeling approach offers significantly higher accuracy with a single and multi-camera settings, across "move-stop-move" events, and re-appearances in a Field of View. Our algorithms offer a high degree of RST (Rotation - Scaling - Translation) invariance and robustness to various noisy conditions.

  • Our technology was developed from research conduced at MIT's Computer Science & Artificial Intelligence Lab (CSAIL) and was designed to automate understanding and “sense-making” of pictures and video in exciting new ways. We also leverage deep learning (convolutional neural networks and recurrent neural networks) to augment and enhance our accuracy.

  • Our patent-pending approach to storing image and motion descriptors uses an "inverted index" which allows for look-ups and look-backs across thousands of hours of video in milliseconds. This proprietary technology is built to be easily scalable for even the largest implementations.

  • We value privacy as much as you do. We can obfuscate the video feed at-source and in real-time, so individuals are de-identified. Similar to how cookies are used on the internet - only aggregated and anonymized information is stored.
